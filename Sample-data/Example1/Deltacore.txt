Deltacore Analytics, an enterprise AI/ML software solutions provider, reported annual revenue of $104 million, a 40% increase supported by five major Fortune 500 client wins. The company boasts a customer retention rate of 92%, a strong indicator of SaaS product reliability. It raised $14 million through a pre-IPO placement, pushing its valuation to $480 million. Deltacore has an Operating Cash Flow (OCF) margin of 14.6%, highlighting good cash efficiency. A distinctive metric is its LTV/CAC ratio of 6.1, a profitability benchmark in SaaS that is not applicable to the other three companies here.
