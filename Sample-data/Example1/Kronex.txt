Kronex InfraTech, an infrastructure development and EPC firm, achieved an EBITDA margin of 23.5% in FY25, backed by successful completion of toll road and metro rail PPP projects. The company monetized mature assets through an Infrastructure Investment Trust (InvIT) structure, raising $255 million. Its asset turnover ratio stands at 1.3, reflecting efficient capital utilization. Kronex also reports a robust order book of $1.19 billion, ensuring steady revenue visibility. A unique operational metric tracked is the Average Project Completion Time, which has improved to 27.4 months, crucial in EPC execution and not used by the others.
